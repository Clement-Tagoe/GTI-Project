Our vision is to train young ladies and women to develop Vocational & Employable Skills

Our Purpose

Our main purpose is to train young ladies and women in Catering & Dressmaking⁄Fashion Designing in order to contribute to;

  • Women's Education & Development
  • Economic Development & Empowerment
  • In fulfillment of God's Kingom Purpose
Our Training Objectives
  • To practically train women to acquire Vocational and Employable Skills
  • To instill basic Godly disciplines, virtues and values into their lives
  • To educate and motivate them to become Productive and Responsible in life
