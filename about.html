Our Main Objective

GTI is a Vocational Training Institution with the main objective of training young ladies and women from the following background;

  • Student and Graduates from Junior and Senior High Schools in Ghana.
  • Those who cannot afford any formal Education and have no profession or vocation that can earn them a living.
  • Other women like workers and housewives who want a second profession or vocation to increase their sources of income.
Our History

The founder and head of the Global Training Institute completed St. Anne's Vocational Training Institute at Odumase Krobo in the Eastern Region of Ghana in 1982 and worked with FLORICA Catering and Dressmaking Services for four years. Mrs. Elizabeth Tagoe (the founder and Proprietress) started her own business namely Global Care-Takers Centre (which is now Global Training Institute) in 1986 with a borrowed Sewing Machine from an elderly female friend, and few Catering Utensils on the corridor of her Mother's Residence at Community 8, Tema.
